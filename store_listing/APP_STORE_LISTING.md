## 8. ASO (App Store Optimization) Tips

1. **First screenshot must stop the scroll** — use bold Arabic calligraphy + screenshot
2. **Use "حلال" in the first 3 words** — it's the top-searched term by your audience
3. **Respond to every review within 24h** — affects ranking
4. **Localize for Saudi Arabia** — largest Muslim app market (SAR pricing above)
5. **Launch during Ramadan** — usage spikes 300%+ for health/fasting apps
6. **"Halal food scanner" gets ~50k searches/month** on App Store globally
